What Does GDSC DO?

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) are university-based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome.

The clubs are aimed at improving students’ development and leadership skills. They offer a range of technical topics and hands-on workshops, events, talks, and project-building activities online and in-person.

About Us


GDSC Society of Halic

The Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) Haliç University is a student-run club that aims to connect students with Google technologies and to promote the development of mobile, web, and cloud applications. The club organizes a variety of events and activities, such as hackathons, workshops, and meetups, to help students learn about Google technologies and to develop their coding skills.

The GDSC Haliç University is open to all students of Haliç University, regardless of their major or skill level. The club has a diverse membership of students from different departments, such as computer science, engineering, and business. The club is led by a team of student leaders who are passionate about technology and helping others learn.
